Modern Format-First

Krynet avoids legacy file types in favor of high-efficiency formats like HEIC, AVIF, HE-AAC, and ZIP. The platform automatically converts or rejects unsupported types, maintaining consistency and optimization. Even supported formats are subject to change as new formats emerge.

Privacy & Policy Enforcement

Krynet enforces rigid policies for user behavior, app integrations ("Applications"), and communities ("Communities"). The platform targets users 16+ and promotes a heavily moderated, encrypted environment.
